UNDERSTANDING YOUR CYCLE

Your hormones, including Estrogen, Progesterone, Luteinizing hormone (LH), and Follicle-stimulating hormone (FSH), play a crucial role in your cycle. They influence your mood, energy, and overall well-being.

🌸

Menstruation

Your period starts and hormone levels are low. Rest and self-care are key.

🌱

Follicular

Estrogen rises, energy increases. Great time for new projects!

☀️

Ovulatory

Peak energy and confidence. Perfect for socializing and connecting.

🍂

Luteal

Progesterone peaks then drops. Focus on self-care and comfort.
